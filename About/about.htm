About Us Find out who makes us tick

How It All Began

Touch Fabric Designer is an initiative by the young minds with a dream to make a significant impact on the Fashion and Apparel Industry of India. T@uch Fabric was incorporated in Jan 2014 with a small workshop in Uttam Nagar region of New Delhi. In this short span of time the company has secured orders from 10+ customers and has 50+ employees. The company is growing with leaps and bounds under the leadership of Mr. Anwar Jamal.

Our Philosophy

Our vision is to take the Indian Garment Industry to new heights by increasing the share of Indian Made Apparels in the International market. We have a customer centric approach and are very particular about Industry Standards and current market trends.

What's In A Name?

T@uch as the name suggests is an aim to revolutionize the Indian Apparel Industry by penetrating through the customers and indulging with their emotions through our products. Our dream is to make a revolution in the personal clothing space and aim to replicate the personal gadget model in the Apparel Industry by rolling out our T@uch Brand Clothing very soon.
